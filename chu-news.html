We Bring You the True News

I created this blog to share with readers 

Scientific findings,

Social issues,

My personal stories and/or observations,

And a bit of my creative fiction writing.

Except for the creative fiction, which will be noted, all the writings are based on scientific research and/or facts.  If they involve personal opinions, they will be specified and the readers can be the judge.

The purpose of heavily relying on science is not to say that religion and personal opinions are not important.  Plus, science is created by humans, and humans are not perfect.  However, the main issue is that science is much more reliable than personal beliefs. 

Why?  There are as many beliefs as the number of humans, and they all think they're right.  Just think, "This must be it and I know I'm right" is not going to make it a fact.  It's good to generate a hypothesis, but then one has to investigate and be willing to be proven wrong if that's the case, 

It's literally impossible to always be right, yet many see themselves that way.  The so-called "common sense" or "intuition" is as reliable as a career politician.

Some might question the validity of science and experts.  And indeed, they can make mistakes.  Actually, that is the point of science: To investigate and correct mistakes in order to find out the truth and facts.  That is why science heavily relies on the peer-review process.  

The fact that we enjoy cars, electricity, architecture, MRI machines, computers, artificial intelligence, etc., etc. is all because of science.  Like democracy, it's not perfect, but it's the best tool we have.

Therefore, in Chu News, we believe a basic understanding of science is critical in modern society.  And thus, we share.
